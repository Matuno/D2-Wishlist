# Warlock Void - Nezarec Soul Siphon

Source confidence: Medium-high. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Warlock data puts Voidwalker fourth at 14.19%, Nova Bomb: Cataclysm at 11.12%, Pocket Singularity at 14.19%, and Feed the Void as the most-used Warlock aspect; Nezarec's Sin is creator-backed but not a top-five Warlock exotic armor usage leader.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long] / [source public DIM](https://dim.gg/nfjik5q/Void)

## Gameplay Loop

Apply Soul Siphon and Void debuffs, then chain Void weapon kills to maintain Abyssal Extractors, Devour, and ability uptime. Use Vortex Grenade for area control and Nova Bomb for burst or boss phases. Keep Feed the Void active by taking kills with abilities or Void weapons.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Nova Bomb: Cataclysm or Vortex
- Class ability: Empowering Rift or Healing Rift
- Melee: Pocket Singularity
- Grenade: Vortex Grenade
- Aspects: Feed the Void, Child of the Old Gods
- Fragments: Echo of Undermining, Echo of Starvation, Echo of Persistence, Echo of Harvest

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Graviton Lance for safe add clear, Collective Obligation for Void debuff storage, or Turncoat if using newer Monument weapon synergies.
- Legendary perks: Repulsor Brace, Destabilizing Rounds, Demolitionist, Attrition Orbs.

## Exotic Armor

Nezarec's Sin is the centerpiece. Update 9.7.0 added suppression synergy to Soul Siphon damage and extra Abyssal Extractors duration from suppressed/Soul Siphon kills.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Shattered Throne armor from the Shattered Throne dungeon for flexible DR/recharge, or Exodus Down for shield-regeneration survival packages.
- Practical mods: Void Siphon, Ashes to Assets, Bolstering Detonation, Firepower, Void Weapon Surge, Recuperation, Bomber.

## Stat Focus

Grenade and Weapons first, then Class. Demolitionist and Powerhouse archetype pieces both work.
