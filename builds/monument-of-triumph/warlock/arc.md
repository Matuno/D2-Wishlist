# Warlock Arc - Geomag Ionic Sentry

Source confidence: High. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Warlock data puts Stormcaller second at 20.16%, Chaos Reach at 14.49%, Ionic Sentry at 17.66%, Storm Grenade at 32.21%, and Geomag Stabilizers as the second-most-used Warlock exotic armor.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Ionic Sentry and Storm Grenades to jolt packs, collect Ionic Traces, and stay Amplified. Geomag Stabilizers turns Chaos Reach into the priority-target answer while the neutral loop feeds Super energy through Arc ability and weapon kills. Play from Healing Rift in GMs; in easier content, use Arc Soul-style tempo and Delicate Tomb/Centrifuse pressure to keep traces flowing.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Chaos Reach or Stormtrance
- Class ability: Healing Rift
- Melee: Ball Lightning or Chain Lightning
- Grenade: Storm Grenade
- Aspects: Ionic Sentry, Electrostatic Mind
- Fragments: Spark of Shock, Spark of Ions, Spark of Resistance, Spark of Amplitude

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Delicate Tomb for Ionic Trace pressure, Anarchy for post-Monument trap damage, or Centrifuse/Thunderlord for pure Arc add clear.
- Legendary perks: Jolting Feedback, Rolling Storm, Voltshot-style perks, Demolitionist.

## Exotic Armor

Geomag Stabilizers are the centerpiece. They align with the Light.gg Chaos Reach usage signal and keep Arc Warlock focused on reliable Super burst instead of only neutral ability uptime.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: SRL armor from Sparrow Racing League for Arc tempo/movement, or Iron Battalion from Iron Banner if Super cycling is the goal.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Ashes to Assets, Firepower, Impact Induction, Arc Weapon Surge, Innervation, Bomber.

## Stat Focus

Super and Grenade first, then Weapons. Demolitionist and Colossus archetype armor are preferred.
