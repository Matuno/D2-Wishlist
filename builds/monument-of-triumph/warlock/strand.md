# Warlock Strand - Swarmers Army

Source confidence: Medium-high. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Warlock data puts Broodweaver fifth at 7.55% and Needlestorm at 7.55%; Swarmers remains the most coherent Strand coverage pick even though it is not a top-five Warlock exotic usage leader.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long] / [source public DIM](https://dim.gg/ahtnrjq/Strand)

## Gameplay Loop

Create Tangles with Strand weapon kills and ability damage, then destroy or throw them to unravel enemies and produce Threadlings. Swarmers makes Threadlings apply Unravel, so every wave of Threadlings becomes more damage and more Tangle pressure. Use Needlestorm for burst or Champion packs.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Needlestorm
- Class ability: Healing Rift
- Melee: Arcane Needle
- Grenade: Threadling Grenade or Shackle Grenade
- Aspects: Mindspun Invocation, Weaver's Call
- Fragments: Thread of Evolution, Thread of Generation, Thread of Warding, Thread of Rebirth

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Final Warning or Buried Bloodline depending on whether you want Strand offense or Devour sustain.
- Recommended legendary: Service of Luzaku or another Strand primary with Hatchling/Demolitionist.
- Perks: Hatchling, Slice, Demolitionist, Attrition Orbs, Pugilist.

## Exotic Armor

Swarmers are the centerpiece. They turn Threadlings into Unravel applicators and make Broodweaver add clear scale into higher density content.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Shattered Throne armor from the Shattered Throne dungeon for flexible DR/recharge, or Seventh Seraph armor if using Threadling/Tangle explosion variants.
- Practical mods: Strand Siphon, Firepower, Impact Induction, Grenade Kickstart, Strand Weapon Surge, Innervation, Bomber.

## Stat Focus

Grenade and Weapons first, then Melee. Demolitionist and Powerhouse archetypes fit the build.
