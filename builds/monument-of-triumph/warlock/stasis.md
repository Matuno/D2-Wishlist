# Warlock Stasis - Rime-Coat Fortress

Source confidence: Medium. Rime-coat/Bleak Watcher is still the premier Stasis Warlock hard-content package, and 9.7.0.2 fixed Rime-coat behavior, but Light.gg Season 29 PvE Warlock data does not put Shadebinder in the top five subclasses; this is a coverage pick.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Charge and throw Bleak Watcher to establish a freezing zone. Cast rift inside or behind the Bleak Domain to fight from safety, then shoot crystals/frozen targets to chain shatters and generate Stasis benefits. Use Iceflare Bolts to spread freezes; save Winter's Wrath for emergency room control.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Winter's Wrath
- Class ability: Healing Rift
- Melee: Penumbral Blast
- Grenade: Coldsnap Grenade or Shatter Grenade
- Aspects: Bleak Watcher, Iceflare Bolts
- Fragments: Whisper of Rime, Whisper of Fissures, Whisper of Shards, Whisper of Torment, Whisper of Conduction

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Ice Breaker for safe precision burst from the turret zone, Ager's Scepter for add clear, or Buried Bloodline for Devour if the fireteam lacks sustain.
- Legendary perks: Headstone, Rimestealer, Chill Clip, Demolitionist.

## Exotic Armor

Rime-coat Raiment is the centerpiece. It upgrades Bleak Watcher into a stronger defensive zone with Stasis crystals and slow/shatter follow-up.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Lustrous armor from Pale Heart sources if using the Solar/Stasis hybrid turret package, or Shattered Throne armor for generic endgame safety.
- Practical mods: Stasis Siphon, Heavy Ammo Finder, Momentum Transfer, Impact Induction, Innervation, Bomber, Reaper.

## Stat Focus

Grenade first, then Super and Weapons. Demolitionist, Siegebreaker, and Colossus armor are the best farms.

## Relic Configuration

Artifact: Queensfoil Censer.

- Fever and Chill
- Kindling Trigger
- Pillar of Ice
- Creeping Chill
- Perpetual Destruction
- Anti-Champion Nosecone
- Hail The Storm
