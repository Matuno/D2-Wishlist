# Warlock Solar - Dawn Chorus Ignition

Source confidence: High. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Warlock data puts Dawnblade third at 15.73%, Song of Flame first among Warlock Supers at 23.98%, Hellion at 18.01%, and Dawn Chorus as the Solar exotic armor usage leader.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Hellion, Solar weapons, and Incinerator Snap to stack scorch quickly, then let Dawn Chorus convert scorch/ignitions into damage and melee energy. Phoenix Dive gives fast repositioning and Hellion uptime, while Song of Flame is the team-safe damage and add-clear Super. Use Healing Grenade when soloing or in GMs; swap to Fusion/Incendiary only when safety is covered.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Song of Flame
- Class ability: Phoenix Dive
- Melee: Incinerator Snap
- Grenade: Healing Grenade
- Aspects: Hellion, Touch of Flame
- Fragments: Ember of Ashes, Ember of Char, Ember of Searing, Ember of Empyrean

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Vex Mythoclast, Dragon's Breath, One Thousand Voices, or Alethonym if the fireteam needs ammo support.
- Recommended legendary: Solar primary with Incandescent or Heal Clip; Cynosure or Apex Predator-style rockets for damage.
- Perks: Heal Clip, Incandescent, Attrition Orbs, Explosive Light, Reconstruction.

## Exotic Armor

Dawn Chorus is the centerpiece. It is the usage-backed Solar Warlock exotic and makes Hellion/Snap/Solar weapon scorch loops scale into real Champion and major damage.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Lustrous armor from Solstice for Solar weapon handling and healing, or Iron Battalion from Iron Banner for Supercyclical in longer team activities.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Power Preservation, Firepower, Impact Induction, Innervation, Absolution, Benevolent Finisher.

## Stat Focus

Grenade and Super first, then Class and Weapons. Demolitionist and Reaver archetypes are the best fit.
