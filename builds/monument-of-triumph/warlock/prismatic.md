# Warlock Prismatic - Getaway Soul Spam

Source confidence: Very high. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Warlock data puts Prismatic Warlock first at 39.09% and Getaway Artist first among Warlock exotic armor at 26.73%, matching current creator and Mobalytics recommendations.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Consume Storm Grenade with Getaway Artist to gain Amplified, Arc Soul, and a Bleak Watcher deployment. Cast Phoenix Dive or Healing Rift to activate Hellion, then use Khvostov or another high-output primary to generate orbs and feed Transcendence. Use Arcane Needle for ability energy, Devour sustain, and Darkness progress. Repeat once the grenade comes back.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Song of Flame
- Class ability: Phoenix Dive or Healing Rift
- Melee: Arcane Needle
- Grenade: Storm Grenade
- Aspects: Bleak Watcher, Hellion
- Fragments: Facet of Grace, Facet of Sacrifice, Facet of Purpose, Facet of Hope, Facet of Dawn, Facet of Devotion

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: Khvostov 7G-0X is recommended for the sourced loop but can be swapped.
- Alternatives: No Time to Explain for another autonomous-damage package, or Buried Bloodline with a Strand/Stasis Demolitionist weapon for Devour/ability uptime.
- Legendary perks: Demolitionist, Attrition Orbs, Hatchling, Incandescent, Voltshot-style perks.

## Exotic Armor

Getaway Artist is the centerpiece. It converts grenade energy into Arc Soul and Amplified, which combines with Bleak Watcher and Hellion into a three-turret style loop.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Lustrous armor from Pale Heart sources for Solar/Stasis hybrid support, or Shattered Throne armor for neutral endgame DR/recharge.
- Practical mods: Kinetic Siphon, Heavy Ammo Finder, Firepower, Impact Induction, Innervation, Insulation, Powerful Attraction.

## Stat Focus

Grenade first, then Class, then Weapons. Demolitionist and Reaver archetype armor are ideal.
