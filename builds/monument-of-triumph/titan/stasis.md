# Titan Stasis - Stronghold Behemoth Guard

Source confidence: High for the subclass lane, medium-high for the exact exotic pairing. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Titan data puts Behemoth second at 20.74%, Glacial Quake at 20.74%, and Stronghold as the third-most-used Titan exotic armor.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Shatter Grenade, Howl of the Storm, and Tectonic Harvest to create shards and crystal cover, then block and counterattack with Praxic Blade or another sword through Stronghold. The loop is defensive first: block to stabilize, shatter crystals to control lanes, then use Glacial Quake when a room needs hard crowd control or burst.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Glacial Quake
- Class ability: Rally Barricade or Towering Barricade
- Melee: Shiver Strike
- Grenade: Shatter Grenade
- Aspects: Howl of the Storm, Tectonic Harvest
- Fragments: Whisper of Conduction, Whisper of Fissures, Whisper of Rending, Whisper of Rime, Whisper of Shards

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None, but Praxic Blade is strongly recommended for the Stronghold loop.
- Alternatives: Ice Breaker for safe precision burst, or Wicked Implement/Verglas Curve for Stasis shard/crystal synergy.
- Legendary perks: Headstone, Rimestealer, Chill Clip, Demolitionist, Eager Edge, Surrounded.

## Exotic Armor

Stronghold is the centerpiece. It pairs with Praxic Blade and the current sword-heavy usage trend to make Behemoth a high-survivability hard-content pick rather than a pure ability-spam setup.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Exodus Down armor from Nessus/Exodus sources for survival-oriented set bonuses, or Shattered Throne armor if you want a neutral DR/recharge package.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Heavy Ammo Finder, Firepower, Focusing Strike, Innervation, Recuperation, Reaper.

## Stat Focus

Health and Weapons first, then Grenade. Colossus and Powerhouse pieces fit the defensive sword profile.
