# Titan Void - No Backup Shotgun Void

Source confidence: Medium. Sentinel is fifth in Light.gg Season 29 PvE Titan usage at 10.65%; this No Backup Plans file is retained as the strongest current Void coverage pick, but it is not a population-leading Titan exotic.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long] / [source public DIM](https://dim.gg/3amqc4y/Void)

## Gameplay Loop

Maintain Void Overshield through Bastion, Shield Throw, and shotgun/melee final blows. No Backup Plans boosts shotguns and melees while a defensive buff is active, so play aggressively only while overshielded or otherwise protected. Use Controlled Demolition to turn volatile detonations into healing and add clear, and save Twilight Arsenal for debuff/burst windows.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Twilight Arsenal
- Class ability: Rally Barricade
- Melee: Shield Throw
- Grenade: Vortex Grenade or Suppressor Grenade
- Aspects: Bastion, Controlled Demolition
- Fragments: Echo of Starvation, Echo of Persistence, Echo of Undermining, Echo of Vigilance

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Conditional Finality for freeze/ignite control, The Chaperone for precision shotgun synergy, or a Void shotgun if matching surges.
- Legendary perks: One-Two Punch, Trench Barrel, Repulsor Brace, Destabilizing Rounds, Attrition Orbs.

## Exotic Armor

No Backup Plans is the centerpiece. It ties shotgun final blows, melee final blows, health regeneration, and defensive buff extension into one loop.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Shattered Throne armor from the Shattered Throne dungeon for endgame DR/recharge, or Smoke Jumper if your group values overshield/DR team play.
- Practical mods: Void Siphon, Heavy Handed, Focusing Strike, Bolstering Detonation, Void Weapon Surge, Reaper, Bomber.

## Stat Focus

Weapons first, then Health and Melee. Skirmisher and Reaver armor both work depending on shotgun vs ability emphasis.
