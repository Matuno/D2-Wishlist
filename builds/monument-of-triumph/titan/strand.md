# Titan Strand - Abeyant Banner Control

Source confidence: Medium-low. Strand Titan remains reliable for suspend/control and Banner of War, but Light.gg Season 29 PvE Titan data does not place Berserker in the top five Titan subclasses; this is a coverage pick.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Drengr's Lash and Shackle Grenade to suspend dangerous packs. Trigger Banner of War through melee, finisher, or sword kills, then keep it rolling with weapon cleanup and team play. Abeyant Leap makes suspend lanes safer and wider; use it to control Champions before committing to melee.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Bladefury
- Class ability: Rally Barricade
- Melee: Frenzied Blade
- Grenade: Shackle Grenade
- Aspects: Drengr's Lash, Banner of War
- Fragments: Thread of Generation, Thread of Warding, Thread of Mind, Thread of Continuity

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: The Navigator for Woven Mail support, or Barrow-Dyad if building into Strand weapon damage.
- Legendary perks: Hatchling, Slice, Pugilist, Demolitionist, Attrition Orbs.

## Exotic Armor

Abeyant Leap is the centerpiece. It upgrades Drengr's Lash into safer hard-control lanes and helps Banner of War Titan stay alive in endgame rooms.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Shattered Throne armor from the Shattered Throne dungeon for generic DR/recharge, or Seventh Seraph armor if using Warmind-style explosion packages.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Heavy Handed, Impact Induction, Focusing Strike, Recuperation, Innervation, Powerful Attraction.

## Stat Focus

Melee and Health first, then Grenade/Weapons. Skirmisher and Reaver archetypes are the most natural farms.
