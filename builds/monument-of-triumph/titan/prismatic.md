# Titan Prismatic - Stoicism Consecration

Source confidence: High. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Titan data puts Prismatic Titan at 18.86% and Stoicism at 8.43%, while current Monument sources converge on Stoicism with Spirit of Inmost Light plus Spirit of Synthoceps or Contact for Prismatic ability loops.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Frenzied Blade and Consecration to ignite packs, then let Spirit of Inmost Light refill abilities and Spirit of Synthoceps or Contact amplify melee damage. Thruster keeps the loop moving, Glacier Grenade adds cover and shatter damage, and Transcendence is for Champion packs or boss add waves. Thundercrash is the burst Super when Cuirass is not equipped.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Thundercrash
- Class ability: Thruster
- Melee: Frenzied Blade
- Grenade: Glacier Grenade
- Aspects: Consecration, Knockout
- Fragments: Facet of Balance, Facet of Purpose, Facet of Ruin, Facet of Protection, Facet of Dawn

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Khvostov 7G-0X for add clear, Praxic Blade if leaning into sword artifact perks, or The Chaperone only for the lower-usage shotgun variant.
- Perks: One-Two Punch, Slice, Hatchling, Headstone, Repulsor Brace, Attrition Orbs.

## Exotic Armor

Stoicism is the centerpiece. Prioritize Spirit of Inmost Light plus Spirit of Synthoceps for the broadest PvE loop; Spirit of Contact is the safer add-clear alternative.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Exodus Down armor from Nessus/Exodus sources for survival-oriented set bonuses, or Iron Battalion from Iron Banner if Super cycling is the priority.
- Practical mods: Kinetic Siphon, Heavy Ammo Finder, Heavy Handed, Grenade Kickstart, Arc Weapon Surge, Time Dilation, Reaper.

## Stat Focus

Melee and Grenade first, then Class and Weapons. Skirmisher and Demolitionist archetype pieces fit best.
