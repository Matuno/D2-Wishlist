# Titan Arc - Cuirass Thundercrash

Source confidence: High. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Titan data puts Striker at 20.71%, Thundercrash at 19.76%, Storm's Keep at 19.57%, and Cuirass of the Falling Star in the top five Titan exotic armor pieces.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Storm's Keep and Pulse Grenades to build Bolt Charge and Ionic Traces, then convert add waves into repeated Thundercrash windows. Thunderclap or Seismic Strike handles close targets, while Cuirass makes Thundercrash the Champion and boss burst button. In harder rooms, play around Rally Barricade and Arc blind/jolt weapons until the Super is ready.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Thundercrash
- Class ability: Rally Barricade
- Melee: Thunderclap or Seismic Strike
- Grenade: Pulse Grenade
- Aspects: Touch of Thunder, Storm's Keep
- Fragments: Spark of Discharge, Spark of Beacons, Spark of Ions, Spark of Frequency

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Anarchy for trap damage after its Monument buff, Centrifuse for Arc blind/add clear, or Delicate Tomb if you want Ionic Trace pressure.
- Legendary perks: Jolting Feedback, Rolling Storm, Voltshot-style perks, Target Lock, Reconstruction.

## Exotic Armor

Cuirass of the Falling Star is the centerpiece. The Light.gg data says Arc Titan usage is centered on Thundercrash, and Cuirass is the exotic that turns that into reliable burst damage.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: SRL armor from Sparrow Racing League for Arc movement/tempo builds, or Iron Battalion from Iron Banner if Thundercrash uptime is the priority.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Heavy Ammo Finder, Firepower, Impact Induction, Arc Weapon Surge, Recuperation, Reaper.

## Stat Focus

Super and Grenade first, then Weapons. Powerhouse and Demolitionist archetype armor fit the burst-and-grenade direction best.
