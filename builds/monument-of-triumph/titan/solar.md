# Titan Solar - Wormgod Sunbreaker

Source confidence: High. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Titan data puts Sunbreaker first at 22.04%, Throwing Hammer at 19.6%, Sol Invictus at 19.52%, Roaring Flames at 17.24%, and Wormgod Caress as the most-used Titan exotic armor.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Throwing Hammer and Solar weapons to stack Roaring Flames, create Sunspots, and build Wormgod Caress melee damage. Step through Sunspots for restoration, then spend the stacked hammer damage on majors and Champions. Use Hammer of Sol for safer add-heavy encounters; swap to Burning Maul/Pyrogale only when a boss burst role matters more than neutral-game uptime.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Hammer of Sol
- Class ability: Rally Barricade
- Melee: Throwing Hammer
- Grenade: Healing Grenade or Thermite Grenade
- Aspects: Sol Invictus, Roaring Flames
- Fragments: Ember of Torches, Ember of Empyrean, Ember of Ashes, Ember of Searing

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Praxic Blade if leaning into the current sword artifact/meta, Dragon's Breath for passive scorch/ignition pressure, or One Thousand Voices if the fireteam is building around Solar burst.
- Legendary perks: Incandescent, Heal Clip, Burning Ambition, Bait and Switch, Explosive Light.

## Exotic Armor

Wormgod Caress is the centerpiece. It is the Light.gg usage leader for Titan PvE and turns Solar hammer play into the highest-upside neutral loop, with Pyrogale kept as a boss-DPS swap.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Iron Battalion from Iron Banner for Supercyclical, especially in add-dense encounters; Bushido from the current World Pool Engram for weapon-heal/DR support.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Heavy Handed, Impact Induction, Firepower, Solar Weapon Surge, Time Dilation, Powerful Attraction.

## Stat Focus

Melee and Super first, then Weapons. Skirmisher and Colossus archetypes are the cleanest fits.
