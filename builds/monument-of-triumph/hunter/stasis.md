# Hunter Stasis - Mask of Fealty Shatter

Source confidence: Medium-high. Revenant is a lower-usage Hunter PvE subclass than Solar/Prismatic/Arc, but Light.gg still shows Revenant at 7.83% and Mask of Fealty as the top Stasis-specific Hunter exotic signal at 5.79%.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Withering Blade to create Stasis crystals and refund melee energy through Mask of Fealty. Freeze or slow groups, break crystals with weapons or Shatterdive, then chain the extra blade eruptions into more slows, freezes, and shard generation. Silence and Squall is the room-control button for Champion packs or revive recovery.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Silence and Squall
- Class ability: Gambler's Dodge
- Melee: Withering Blade
- Grenade: Shatter Grenade or Coldsnap Grenade
- Aspects: Grim Harvest, Shatterdive
- Fragments: Whisper of Fissures, Whisper of Shards, Whisper of Rime, Whisper of Conduction

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Ice Breaker for safe burst, Salvation's Grip for crystal-heavy rooms, or Buried Bloodline for Devour sustain.
- Legendary perks: Headstone, Rimestealer, Chill Clip, Demolitionist.

## Exotic Armor

Mask of Fealty is the centerpiece. Withering Blade hits and crystal shatters create more Stasis pressure and refund melee energy, making the loop much more active than passive cooldown exotics.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Shattered Throne armor from the Shattered Throne dungeon for the most flexible endgame DR and recharge loop.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Grenade Kickstart, Impact Induction, Elemental Charge, Innervation, Absolution, Bomber.

## Stat Focus

Grenade first, then Weapons, then Class. Demolitionist and Siegebreaker archetypes are best if available.
