# Hunter Void - Gyrfalcon Phantom

Source confidence: Medium-high. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Hunter data puts Nightstalker fourth at 13.13% and Phantom Surge at 7.48%; Gyrfalcon's Hauberk is creator-backed but not a top-five Hunter exotic armor usage leader.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long] / [source public DIM](https://dim.gg/ilfadba/Void)

## Gameplay Loop

Use Vanishing Step or Stylish Executioner to enter invisibility, then break invisibility to trigger Gyrfalcon volatile rounds. Chain volatile kills with Graviton Lance, Turncoat, or a Repulsor Brace/Destabilizing Rounds weapon. Dodge to refresh invisibility, use smoke to weaken or escape, and fire Shadowshot for boss debuff windows or dangerous Champion packs.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Shadowshot: Deadfall or Moebius Quiver
- Class ability: Marksman's Dodge
- Melee: Snare Bomb
- Grenade: Vortex Grenade
- Aspects: Stylish Executioner, Vanishing Step
- Fragments: Echo of Starvation, Echo of Persistence, Echo of Undermining, Echo of Obscurity, Echo of Harvest

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Graviton Lance for safe volatile chains; Turncoat if using the Phantom Surge style and wanting newer Monument weapon synergy.
- Legendary perks: Repulsor Brace, Destabilizing Rounds, Attrition Orbs, Demolitionist.

## Exotic Armor

Gyrfalcon's Hauberk is the centerpiece. It converts invisibility exits into volatile rounds and lets Void weapons carry both add clear and defensive overshield loops.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Shattered Throne armor from the Shattered Throne dungeon. Truth to Power was fixed in 9.7.0.1 and is now a strong generic endgame DR/ability-recharge package.
- Practical mods: Void Siphon, Heavy Ammo Finder, Focusing Strike, Bolstering Detonation, Void Weapon Surge, Reaper, Powerful Attraction.

## Stat Focus

Weapons first, then Class, then Grenade. Reaver and Powerhouse armor archetypes both fit.
