# Hunter Prismatic - Cyrtarachne GM Champion

Source confidence: High. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Hunter data puts Prismatic Hunter second at 25.82% and Relativism third among Hunter exotic armor at 10.54%, matching current GM Champion-style Prismatic Hunter recommendations.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long] / [source public DIM](https://dim.gg/d2dwroy/Prismatic)

## Gameplay Loop

Open with Duskfield or Threaded Spike to debuff and control targets. Use grenade casts for Woven Mail through Spirit of Cyrtarachne, then dodge for melee refunds and decoys. Gunpowder Gamble handles Unstoppable/add-clear bursts, while Threaded Spike applies Sever and feeds Darkness energy. Use Transcendence for Champion packs or boss pressure.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Silence and Squall, Deadfall, or Golden Gun depending on the encounter
- Class ability: Acrobat's Dodge or Gambler's Dodge
- Melee: Threaded Spike
- Grenade: Duskfield Grenade
- Aspects: Gunpowder Gamble, Threaded Specter
- Fragments: Facet of Awakening, Facet of Devotion, Facet of Honor, Facet of Ruin, Facet of Protection, Facet of Grace

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended: Kinetic primary with strong Champion coverage, a Strand/Void weapon to exploit Facet of Bravery-style volatile/unravel options if you swap fragments, and a burst heavy for bosses.
- Perks: Demolitionist, Attrition Orbs, Repulsor Brace, Hatchling, Slice.

## Exotic Armor

Relativism is the centerpiece. Preferred rolls are Spirit of Renewal + Spirit of Cyrtarachne for layered Frost Armor/Woven Mail, or Spirit of Inmost Light + Spirit of Cyrtarachne for faster ability cycling.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Shattered Throne armor from the Shattered Throne dungeon for general endgame DR/recharge. Use Iron Battalion if you are building around frequent Super use.
- Practical mods: Kinetic Siphon, Heavy Ammo Finder, Grenade Kickstart, Impact Induction, Elemental Charge, Recuperation, Reaper, Bomber.

## Stat Focus

Grenade and Class first, then Melee and Weapons. Demolitionist or Reaver archetype armor is preferred.
