# Hunter Arc - Shinobu Arcworks

Source confidence: High. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Hunter data puts Arcstrider third at 19.72%, Shinobu's Vow second among Hunter exotic armor at 10.64%, Skip Grenade at 11.81%, and Ascension/Tempest Strike in the top Hunter aspect set.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Build Bolt Charge with Skip Grenade hits, Arc weapon kills, and Disorienting Blow. Use Ascension to keep the team Amplified, then throw enhanced Skip Grenades into dense packs to heal nearby allies and jolt/blind targets. Gambler's Dodge restores melee for another Bolt Charge source. Gathering Storm is the boss/Champion punish button.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Gathering Storm
- Class ability: Gambler's Dodge
- Melee: Disorienting Blow
- Grenade: Skip Grenade
- Aspects: Ascension, Tempest Strike
- Fragments: Spark of Resistance, Spark of Shock, Spark of Ions, Spark of Frequency, Spark of Amplitude

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended: Arc primary or special with Rolling Storm, Jolting Feedback, or Voltshot-style uptime. Antedate and other Arc primaries are good neutral picks.
- Heavy: Thunderlord, Bitter End, or an Arc machine gun for sustained add clear and ammo-friendly Champion work.

## Exotic Armor

Shinobu's Vow is the center of the build. It adds a second Skip Grenade charge and converts Skip Grenade/Bolt Charge uptime into healing and stronger grenade output.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: SRL armor from Sparrow Racing League if leaning into movement and Arc tempo, or Shattered Throne armor from the Shattered Throne dungeon for generic endgame DR/ability recharge.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Firepower, Momentum Transfer, Grenade Font, Recuperation, Innervation, Bomber.

## Stat Focus

Grenade first, then Weapons, then Class. Use Demolitionist or Siegebreaker archetype pieces if you are farming Armor 3.0 drops.
