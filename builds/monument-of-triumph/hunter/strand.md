# Hunter Strand - Moirai Beyblades

Source confidence: Medium. Strand Hunter is a complete-coverage pick rather than a Light.gg top-usage pick; Season 29 PvE Hunter data shows Grapple and Threaded Spike usage, but Threadrunner is below the top five subclass list.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long] / [source public DIM](https://dim.gg/x4p3baa/Strand-+-Arc)

## Gameplay Loop

Create a Tangle with Threaded Spike, Strand weapon kills, or suspended targets. Throw Threaded Spike into the Tangle to detonate it, spawn Whirling Maelstrom, and use Moirai to refund the melee while duplicating or extending the Tangle chain. Grapple to your Tangles for mobility, Woven Mail uptime, and safe repositioning.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Silkstrike
- Class ability: Gambler's Dodge
- Melee: Threaded Spike
- Grenade: Grapple or Shackle Grenade
- Aspects: Whirling Maelstrom, Widow's Silk
- Fragments: Thread of Generation, Thread of Warding, Thread of Mind, Thread of Continuity

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: The Navigator if the team values Woven Mail support; otherwise use a Strand primary or sidearm.
- Legendary perks: Hatchling, Slice, Demolitionist, Pugilist, Attrition Orbs.

## Exotic Armor

Moirai is the centerpiece. It lets Threaded Spike interact with Tangles, detonating and rewiring them into repeatable melee/Tangle loops.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Seventh Seraph armor if using the Warmind-style explosion package shown in current Moirai variants, or Shattered Throne armor if you want the safer generic endgame package.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Heavy Handed, Focusing Strike, Impact Induction, Better Already, Stacks on Stacks, Reaper.

## Stat Focus

Weapons first, then Grenade, then Class/Melee. Skirmisher or Reaver archetype pieces are the easiest fit.

## Relic Configuration
