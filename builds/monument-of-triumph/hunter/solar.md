# Hunter Solar - Infinite Crackshot

Source confidence: High. Light.gg Season 29 PvE Hunter data puts Gunslinger first at 29.66%, Speedloader Slacks first among Hunter exotic armor at 15.83%, and Crackshot/On Your Mark among the most-used Hunter aspects.

DIM: [Long-form import][dim-long].

## Gameplay Loop

Use Crackshot to become Radiant, then keep dealing Solar weapon damage to refund class ability energy through Speedloader Slacks and Ember of Singeing. Healing Grenade starts Restoration, On Your Mark keeps reload and handling high, and precision Solar kills trigger scorch/ignition chains through the artifact. Blade Barrage is the default burst Super; swap to Golden Gun/Celestial only for planned boss DPS phases.

## Subclass Setup

- Super: Blade Barrage
- Class ability: Acrobat's Dodge
- Melee: Knife Trick
- Grenade: Healing Grenade
- Aspects: Crackshot, On Your Mark
- Fragments: Ember of Singeing, Ember of Empyrean, Ember of Ashes, Ember of Torches, Ember of Solace

## Weapons

- Required exotic weapon: None.
- Recommended exotic: Vex Mythoclast, Sunshot, Skyburner's Oath, Dragon's Breath, or Still Hunt as a boss-damage swap.
- Recommended legendary weapons: Solar primary with Incandescent, Heal Clip, or Attrition Orbs; Strand or Kinetic slot with Slice, Demolitionist, or area denial; bait-and-switch or Explosive Light heavy.

## Exotic Armor

Speedloader Slacks are the centerpiece. Sustained damage refills dodge energy and improves reload/handling, which lets Crackshot and Solar weapon scorch loops stay active in hard content without requiring kill chains.

## Armor Set And Mods

- Recommended set: Lustrous from Solstice for Solar weapon handling and rapid Solar final-blow healing; Bushido from the current World Pool Engram if running bow/sword/shotgun-sidearm pairings.
- Practical mods: Harmonic Siphon, Heavy Ammo Finder, Solar Weapon Surge, Time Dilation, Reaper, Powerful Attraction.

## Stat Focus

Class and Weapons first, then Grenade. Skirmisher and Reaver archetype armor fit the constant dodge/weapon loop best.
